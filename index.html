Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial intelligence (AI) is an area of computer science that emphasizes the creation of intelligent machines that work and react like humans. Some of the activities computers with artificial intelligence are designed for include: Speech recognition, Learning, Planning, Problem solving, BOTs.

Using algorithms that iteratively learn from data, machine learning allows computers to learn without being explicitly programmed where to look. The result? High-value predictions that can guide better decisions and smart actions in real time without human intervention.
